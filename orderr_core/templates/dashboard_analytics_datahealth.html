A split is one real customer stored as two OrdeRR rows: an invoice record (holds the Vasy sales invoices → revenue) and an AR record (holds the outstanding & receipts + the phone/salesperson). It happens when the Vasy sales-invoice export names the party differently from the customer master (e.g. PATILVADA vs PATILVADA HOTEL) and carries no mobile, so the importer can't link the invoices to the phone-bearing record. Pairs below are matched by name prefix. Merge moves the invoices/revenue onto the AR record, records a name alias so future imports auto-link (no re-split), and deletes the duplicate — the permanent one-time fix. Review each pair before merging.
