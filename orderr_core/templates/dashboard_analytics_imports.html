❓ How uploads work — do I upload daily?
Two kinds of files, two behaviours:
  • Ledgers (Receipts, Sales Invoices, Purchases, Expenses, Payments) upsert on their document number — cumulative & idempotent. Any range works (a day, a month, a year); re-uploading overlaps never duplicates. The DB keeps the union of everything uploaded.
  • Outstanding is a daily snapshot stamped today — it refreshes each customer's current balance and records one dated snapshot. Trend / rising-falling / sharper credit scores need ≥2 different days, so upload it once every day.
Recommended routine:
  • One-time backfill: upload the widest date range Vasy allows for the 5 ledgers — history populates in one shot.
  • Then daily: export yesterday (overlap is safe) for all six & upload; Outstanding especially, every day.
  • For a correct P&L, keep Sales Invoices, Purchases & Expenses over the same date range — otherwise the Financials page shows its coverage warning.
